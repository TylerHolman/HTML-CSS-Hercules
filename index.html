Hercules (1997)

Overview

This is a movie about a demi-god that is the son of the God Zeus. He will face many hardships due to the jealousy of Hera.


Hades found of a prophecy saying that Hercules will kill him, which drives him mad and attempts to kill Hercules multiple times.

Characters

Hercules - Demi-God son of Zeus and main protaganist of the movie.

Megara - is Hercules' love interest and is a slave to Hades.

Zeus - is the King of the Gods and father of Hercules.

Hades - brother to Zeus and main antagonist of the movie.

Hera - wife to Zeus, Queen of the Gods, and alleged mother of Hercules.

Phil - was a failed Hero trainer, who took on Hercules.
